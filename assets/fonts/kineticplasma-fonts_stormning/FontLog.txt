Medieval & Dalecarlian Runes by Mew Too

Pentimal Numeral Runes by Robert Jablonski & Mew Too

All characters in this font are Nordic Runes from the Elder Futhark, Younger Futhark, Scandinavian Medieval Runic alphabet, Norse Pentimal Numeric system and Swedish Dalecarlian Runic alphabet.

All Characters are equivalent to Latin characters.
